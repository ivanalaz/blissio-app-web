Design a single-page landing page for a SaaS product called Studyo — a studio management app built specifically for dance clubs in Serbia (contemporary and jazz ballet, competitive clubs). The target user is a dance studio owner or club administrator, aged 28–50, who currently manages everything through Viber groups, Google Sheets, and cash payments. The page is in Serbian language. Visual style: Dark, editorial, confident. Background is near-black (#0D0C0B). Primary text is warm off-white (#F0EDE6). Accent color is a deep amber (#D4820A) used sparingly — only for CTAs, key numbers, and hover states. Secondary surfaces use a very dark warm gray (#1A1917). No gradients. No stock photos of dancers. No hero illustration. Typography is the hero — large, bold, expressive headlines in a modern grotesque typeface (use Inter or DM Sans, weight 700 for headlines, 400 for body). Generous whitespace. Thin 0.5px borders in rgba(255,255,255,0.1) for card separators. Border-radius 8px on cards and buttons. The overall feeling should be: this was made by someone who understands dance clubs, not a generic SaaS company. Page structure and copy — implement exactly as written: 1.
